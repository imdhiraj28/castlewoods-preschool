✨ About Us

Nurturing Curiosity, Creativity & Confidence Since 2013

Castlewood International Pre-School has been a trusted name in early childhood education in Indore for over a decade. We believe every child is unique, and our approach combines structured learning with play-based activities to help children develop academically, emotionally, and physically.

Our caring teachers, safe campus, and enriching programs create an environment where children feel valued, confident, and excited to learn every day.
